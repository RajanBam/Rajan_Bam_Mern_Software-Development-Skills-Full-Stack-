## What the video shows

1. **Landing page** — the Apple-style marketing page, light/dark toggle, scroll.
2. **Sign up / log in** — creating an account (JWT auth).
3. **Dashboard** — creating a new board.
4. **Drawing** — hand-drawn rectangles, ellipses, diamonds, arrows, freehand pen,
   text, sticky notes, stickers; changing crayon colours and stroke widths.
5. **Smart arrows** — dragging a shape and watching bound arrows follow it.
6. **Real-time collaboration** — a second browser window on the same board, with
   live cursors and strokes syncing between them.
7. **Laser pointer** — presenting with the glowing trail.
8. **Export & share** — exporting a PNG and opening the public read-only link.
